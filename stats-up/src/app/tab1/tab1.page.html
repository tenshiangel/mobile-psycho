 Continue with Nino Nakano

Nino Nakano (中なか野の 二に乃の, Nakano Nino) is the second sister of the Nakano quintuplets. She usually acts as the mother of the family, cooks for them and takes care of their medical needs. Among the five sisters, she is the one who has the highest hostility towards Fuutarou Uesugi and often rebels against him early in the series
